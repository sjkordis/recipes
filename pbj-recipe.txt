Ingredients:
 - 2 tbsp peanut butter
 - 2 slices whole grain bread
 - 2 tbsp apricot jam

Directions:
  1. Spread the peanut butter on one slice of the bread.
  2. Spread the jam on the other slice of bread.
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together, peanut butter side to jam side.
  4. Slice diagonally into two triangles.
